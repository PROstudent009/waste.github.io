What is SWACHHATA?

    About The Swachhata Project is an ambitious initiative aimed at promoting cleanliness and sanitation by focusing on the major issues of waste.

    The company strives to meet the needs of the citizens who are unable to communicate with higher authorities.

    The Swachhata project is created from the ideas of renowned students of Marathwada Mitramandal's College of Engineering,Pune.

    Pune city as a smart city develops initiative for the empowerment and constant participation of youth in awareness of the waste management in the city.

    Goal. The goal of SWACHHATA is to improve efficiency, sustainability, cost effectiveness in waste collection, disposal & recycling process.

    The primary goal of waste management planning is to prepare a community to effectively manage waste, debris and materials generated by a homeland security incident, including reducing the potential amount of waste generated at the outset.

WHAT ROLE DO YOU PLAY?

  • Government Organizing waste collection services under smart city waste management projects initialized by the Pune Municipal Corporation.
  • Private Industries. Private companies bring expertise, resources & efficiency to waste management, contributing to the development of sustainable & environmentally responsible solutions.
  • Citizen. To bring the attention of the government toward the waste at public places

OUR SERVICES

  • Waste Collection To allow citizens to report full dustbins through image upload and navigation for timely collection of waste.
  • Credit Points The citizen can submit valid information about waste related issues such as reporting overflowing bins or illegal dumping sites by registering to the website.
  • Event Organization Regular communication with stakeholders and sponsors to provide details of the past as well the upcoming events related to Smart Waste Management.
  • Awareness Enage local communities and stakeholders, in the awareness spreading process by organizing community meetings to address queries and build the sense of ownership and resposibility.
